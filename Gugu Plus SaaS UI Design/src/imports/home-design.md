Design the Home screen for a modern web SaaS called “Gugu Plus”.

Product identity:
Personal price history tracking tool.
Minimal SaaS dashboard.
Data-focused.
Not an e-commerce store.

Design inspiration:
Linear, Notion, Stripe dashboard.

-----------------------------------

LAYOUT

Left sidebar navigation:

Home
Tracked Items
Notifications
Settings

Main content area: Home dashboard.

Soft neutral background.
Card-based layout.

-----------------------------------

SECTION 1 — Personalized Picks

Based on user-selected interests during signup.

Example interests:
Mechanical Keyboards
Sneakers
Apple Accessories
Headphones

Display product cards in a grid.

Each card includes:
- Product image
- Product name
- Marketplace badge
- Current price
- 7-day price change indicator

Cards should look like data cards, not shopping ads.

-----------------------------------

SECTION 2 — Trending Price Drops

Show products with the biggest recent price drops.

Card includes:
- Product image
- Current price
- Previous price
- Drop percentage

Label:
“Trending Price Drops”

-----------------------------------

SECTION 3 — Most Tracked Items

Show popular products tracked by many users.

Each card includes:
- Product image
- Product title
- Current price
- Number of users tracking

Label:
“Most Tracked Items”

-----------------------------------

SECTION 4 — Your Tracking Summary

Small dashboard card showing:

- Items currently tracked
- Last price update time
- Price drops detected this week

Example text:

“You are tracking 3 items.
Last update: today.
2 price drops detected this week.”

-----------------------------------

VISUAL STYLE

Minimal SaaS aesthetic.

Neutral background.
Rounded cards.
Soft shadows.
Modern sans-serif typography.

Accent color:
Subtle blue or green.

No shopping banners.
No discount badges.
No aggressive marketing visuals.

The UI must feel like a data dashboard,
not an e-commerce marketplace.

-----------------------------------

DESIGN PRINCIPLES

Exploration, not shopping.
Data first.
Minimal distractions.
Clean information hierarchy.
